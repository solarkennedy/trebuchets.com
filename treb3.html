Trebuchet 3:

Trebuchet 3 was quite a bomb. Our base uses railroad ties. In the above picture, we used a 20' long boom. We used the box from trebuchet 2. Recently, the boom has broken because of the excessive torque. Our second boom was indestructable, yet too short. It did not send the projectile flying very far. A launch of about 50 feet was very dissapointing. It has been dismanteled.
