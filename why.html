Many a time we have been asked the question: "Yea, but why?" Well the answer is, in simple terms, "Because its cool!" A more precise answer can be described as follows....

Here in our small town of Land O' Lakes, there is not much to do. There is no movie theater, we have a small library, and no night clubs. As 16 year old guys, we must do something to challenge our minds and bodies. It provides for entertainment, watching huge projectiles fly. It also provides a challenge, not only is it physically stressful, but constant problems arise that require us to use our minds to their fullest potential. Building Trebuchets is more than a hobby, it is a passion.
