The Sling

The sling usually comes in two different styles. First, you have the permanent sling. This is essentially a rope attached to your projectile. When the rope releases off of the Trebuchet, the rope flies along with your projectile. The second idea is more complex. It relies on two strings and a bag. One string is permanently attached to the boom, the other is on the release mechanism. Both of these strings are attached to the bag. When one string releases, the bag opens, and the projectile flies out.

The Release Mechanism

There really are two release mechanism on a Trebuchet. The first one let's the boom go and sends the Trebuchet in motion. The second releases the sling, and frees the projectile.

The Box

The Box is a simple, yet critical component of the Trebuchet. Again you want to make as robust as a box as you can. You want to make sure that the box is sturdy enough to hold your desired counterweight.
