Trebuchet 2:

Trebuchet 2 was the first decent trebuchet. It's maximum throwing length was 200 feet, with an orange. We accomplished this using around 250lbs of counter weight. After many revisions, we finally achieved the 200' mark. Trebuchet 2 ended with its disgraceful destruction when we tried to prop the counter weight. The box went on to be used in Trebuchet 3. For counterweight, we used mostly water and concrete blocks. We used a piece of 1/2 inch rebar for the axle.
